About me

Hi! Welcome to My Site!

I am Mahfuzul Nissan. Currently, I am doing my Ph.D. in Engineering & Applied Sciece - Computer Science. I have started my PhD journey from Spring, 2021 in the Department of Computer Science at the University of New Orleans (UNO) - New Orleans, Louisiana, USA. Also, I am working as a Graduate Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. James Wagner.

My research interests include (but not limited to) the followings:

  • Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity
  • Digital Forensics
  • Database Security

I often integrate machine learning tools and techniques to solve the cybersecurity related problems. Currently, I am working on developing novel solutions for detecting insider threats using machine learning (Funded by Louisiana Board of Regents).

I have obtained M.Sc. in Computer Science from University of New Orleans, New Orleans, Louisiana, USA in December 2022. The title of my thesis is "Analysis of Forensic Artifacts in Database Memory using Support Vector Machine".

Prior to joining at UNO, I worked in IT industry and multinational company. During that time, I was fortunate enough to work in several mega projects of Bangladesh.

I have completed Bachelor of Computer Science from International Islamic University, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia in February 2017. I worked under Dr. Rizal Mohd. Nor for my final year project for 6 months from January 2016 to June 2016.

You can explore the details about me throughout this website. Feel free to contact me for any kind of inquiries.
